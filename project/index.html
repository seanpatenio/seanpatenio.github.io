About Niantic

Niantic, Inc. is a leading augmented reality (AR) technology company known for creating engaging mobile games. The company's vision is to create technology that encourages real-world exploration and social interaction. Niantic's platform enables developers to build immersive, location-based AR experiences, making the real world a place of play, exploration, and connection.

About Nintendo

Nintendo Co., Ltd. is a world-renowned gaming company known for creating beloved franchises like Pokémon, Mario, and Zelda. Nintendo has a long history of innovation in the gaming industry, providing unique gaming experiences across various platforms. As a collaborator in the Pokémon GO project, Nintendo continues to bring its rich heritage of fun and adventure to players worldwide.
